About Me

Dr. Obianuju Okafor

I was born and raised in Nigeria. I left at the age of 17 to pursue a college education at Eastern Mediterranean University in Famagusta, North Cyprus. After 4 years, I graduated top of my class with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. Upon graduation, I decided to further my education by going to graduate school. I moved to the US to attend the University of North Texas where I earned a Master's and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science.

Since graduating I have had several roles such as an R&D Software Engineer at Microsoft on the Azure Data Org, and an Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Texas at Austin. My areas of expertise include Software Engineering, Human-Computer Interaction, and Accessibility.

I'm passionate about creating easy-to-use and accessible technology, increasing the participation of marginalized communities in computing, mentoring young people coming up in the tech industry, teaching and imparting knowledge to the next generation, public speaking, and giving back to the community.

In my free time, I like to listen to podcasts and audiobooks, work out, learn new languages, play word games, explore new places, and do DIY projects in my home.

My friends would describe me as kind, intelligent, courageous, resilient, and multifaceted!

Services

I offer the following services:

Application Development

I create robust, user-friendly, and accessible websites. My services encompass the fullstack web development, from front-end design using HTML, CSS, and React, to back-end development using Node, Express, and MySQL/MongoDB.

UX Research

My services revolve around understanding user behavior, needs, and preferences to enhance the user interface design, accessibility, and overall user experience of digital products or services.

Consulting

I provide strategic guidance and solutions that align with business objectives. This includes technology assessments, where I analyze existing systems to identify inefficiencies and recommend optimizations.

Speaking Engagement

I offer engaging and insightful presentations tailored to various audiences and themes. My services include keynote speeches, workshops, and seminars focused on diverse topics such as creating accessible web applications, continuous learning, personal development, and more.

Brand Management

I provide a suite of services aimed at enhancing and optimizing brand identity, perception, and engagement. I perform strategic brand development, starting from comprehensive brand audits and analysis to define or refine brand positioning/personality, values, and messaging, to wardrobe styling.

Mentorship

I guide and support individuals in their personal and professional development journeys to maximize their potential. I offer 1:1 coaching sessions focused on identifying strengths and areas for growth, setting goals, creating actionable plans for success, and resources to help them succeed
