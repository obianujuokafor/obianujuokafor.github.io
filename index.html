Passions

Along with being a full time student and researcher, i have several things that i am passionate about, some of them include:

Goals

1 - Empowering women in STEM

I am zealous about promoting and encouraging underrepresented minority women in science and technology fields, in particular women of color in STEM in the United States and Nigeria. I currently volunteer as a mentor and instructor in several organizations aimed at promoting underrepresented minority women in STEM.

2 - Fashion and Technology

Another interest of mine is fashion and technology. I have always had a flare for fashion and that coupled with me being in the technology field has made me particularly interested in the Fashion-Tech field. My knowledge of the two has landed me internships in companies like Macy's, BloomingDale and Nordstrom as a Software Engineer.

3 - Travelling

I am also passionate about traveling. I enjoy visiting different countries, learning about different cultures and interacting with people of different backgrounds. It broadens my outlook on life and makes me aware of the importance of diversity. As a PhD student i get to travel a lot and attend various conferences.
