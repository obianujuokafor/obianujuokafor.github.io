Passions

Goals

Passions

Along with being a full time student and researcher, i have several things that i am passionate about, some of them include:

Goals

1 - Empowering females in STEM

I am zealous about promoting and encouraging underrepresented minority women and girls in science and technology fields, in particular women of color in STEM in the United States and Nigeria. I currently volunteer as a mentor and instructor in several organizations aimed at promoting underrepresented minority women in STEM.

2 - Increasing accessibility for People with disabilities in CS

I am interested in making computer science education and programming more accessible for people with disabilities. My research has focused on creating an accessible programming environment for people with visual and motor impairments via the block-based programming language Blockly, using screen readers, keyboard shortcuts and speech recognition.

3 - Using data to solve every day problems

Another interest of mine is the use of data and machine learning techniques to solve interesing problems or automate mundane tasks. Some problems I have worked on include: chatbots, recommendation systems, automatic banner design generation, automatic theme/topic analysis and automatic machine learning pipeline generation.
